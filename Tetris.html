Tetris October 2017

Description

Tetris is an iOS app that has the user swipe the screen left and right to move the falling blocks into the ideal location, and tapping the screen rotates the block. It follows the classic rules of tetris in that if there is no where else to spawn a block at the top of the screen, then the player loses. This app has the tetris theme song playing the whole time, and an announcer will say which piece has just spawned. This was a good challenge as it incorporated an interesting use of data structures.
